FAQ

At Winter of Code, we strive to bring together developers to participate in the hackathon irrespective of gender, race, sexual orientation, socioeconomic background or ethnicity. We usually try to keep things simple. Read our FAQs to phase out ambiguity.

Is this competition only for the first years?
No. The competition is open to all the students of IIT (ISM), Dhanbad.
Can I get some resources?
You can get resources related to the event in the resources section this website.
Is this a team event or an individual event?
Winter of Code is an individual event.
I have no prior experience in open source software, can I still participate?
Sure! Open source software (OSS) is all about learning and developing. Everyone starts from the basics and leads themselves to contributing more. No matter if you are a newbie or and experienced coder. Winter of Code is having something for you.
Is there any fees for participating in the Winter of code comptetion?
No! All you have to do is register and get coding!
How does the application process work?
For first years they will contribute to the projects under Cyber lab members after submitting a proper proposal of what they will be doing. For second years and above they should contribute through solving current issues in the projects and sending pull request.
What should be the manner or process of thinking?
Analyse the issue in your project properly and try to resolve it the way you think is better. It's not just doing anything and getting the results.
What will I get after participating in Winter Of Code?
Experience and learning. Having things done perfectly or partially in a specified manner is much better than not doing anything.OSS is a wide field and with this you all can get a feel of what GSOC is. Plus in such furthur competitions you will have a glimpse of the environment.Also exciting goodies are waiting for you.
